Austin Council of the Blind Presents

"Dining in the Dark"

Join us!

Friday, May 22nd, 2015, 6:45pm; Cash Bar - 5:45pm

Crown Plaza Hotel: Get Directions

6121 North I-35 Austin, Texas 78752

$75 Per Person

A Journey of Tastes, Sounds, and Touch

Guests will dine on a four course gourmet meal in a darkened banquet room. Members of the Austin Council of the Blind will be serving you. Tickets may be purchased online or contact Kevin Ratliff (512) 348-7721 for more information.

The Austin Council of the Blind is a 501c3 Organization Dedicated to Advocacy, meeting the needs of and promoting awareness with regards to individuals who are Blind or Visually impaired. The mission of the Austin Council of the Blind is to provide educational and informational programs and improve accessibility and independence for the blind and visually impaired.
