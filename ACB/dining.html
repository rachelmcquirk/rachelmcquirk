The Austin Council of the Blind is a 501c3 Organization Dedicated to Advocacy, meeting the needs of and promoting awareness with regards to individuals who are Blind or Visually impaired. The mission of the Austin Council of the Blind is to provide educational and informational programs and improve accessibility and independence for the blind and visually impaired.
