Our Mission

The purpose of Austin Council of the Blind is to provide educational and informational programs and improve accessibility and independence for the blind and visually impaired. Things ACB does:

  • Support Services for the Blind
  • Scholarships for Blind Students
  • Braille Equipment
  • Mobility Canes
  • Newsletters
  • Educational Programs
  • Children Braille Books
  • Advocacy
  • Radio Reading Service
  • Referral Services
