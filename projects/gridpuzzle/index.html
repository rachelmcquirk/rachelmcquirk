Grid Puzzle

The Puzzle

  1. There are 5 houses in five different colors.
  2. In each house lives a person with a different name.
  3. These five owners have a favorite fruit, favorite physical activity and have a certain dog breed (except for one, who has a cat).
  4. No owners have the same dog breed, or the same favorite physical activity or the same favorite fruit.

To win you must correctly fill in the grid and answer the following question.

Hints:

  • Sally has a Samoyed
  • Jordan loves mangos
  • the orange house is on the left of the red house
  • the orange house's owner loves strawberries
  • the person who goes hiking has a sheltie
  • the owner of the green house does yoga
  • the person living in the center house favorites pineapples
  • Hansel lives in the first house
  • the person who weight lifts lives next to the one who has a husky
  • the person who has a cat (rather than an awesome dog) lives next to the person who does yoga
  • the owner who snowboards loves coconuts
  • Bradley plays tennis
  • Hansel lives next to the blue house
  • the person who weight lifts has a neighbor who loves apples
