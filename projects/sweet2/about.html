Our Story

Sweet nuTREATion has been a vision of ours for the past 5 years, longer if you count the first day that any of us wished for a nutritious desert that retained its decadence. After a lot of hard work, we opened our doors in February of 2015, serving nutritious treats to the sweet-tooths of thousands.

Our Team

Kelsey Koger, Founder

Kelsey is the woman behind the magic. She has her nutrition degree from The University of Texas and her passion for nutrition led her to develop a formula for our healthy desserts. She wants to spread her nutritionally-balanced desserts in hopes of bettering America's nutrition. In her spare time Kelsey loves to workout, spend time with friends, and watch this music video.

Cookie Monster, Co-founder

Cookie Monster is our amazing baker and social media extraordinaire. He believes every person should be empowered to eat sweets daily - he himself has always wanted to eat cookies for every meal. When Kelsey came to him with her concerns about his nutrition, he decided to become her cofounder/head baker so that he could eat all of the cookies he wanted without listening to Kelsey's concerns any more.
